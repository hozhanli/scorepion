Frequently asked questions

What is Scorepion?

Scorepion is a free football score-prediction game. Predict match results, earn points, build streaks, climb the leaderboard, and compete with friends in groups. It's for entertainment — there's no betting or real-money wagering.

How do I create an account?

Open the app and sign up with your email address and a password. You'll choose a username that appears on the leaderboard.

I didn't receive my password-reset email.

Reset emails are sent from noreply@scorepion-7b110.firebaseapp.com and can land in your Spam or Promotions folder — please check there and mark it "Not spam". Make sure you used the same email you registered with. If it still doesn't arrive, email support@scorepion.fans and we'll help.

How are predictions scored?

Predictions are settled automatically against official match results from our data provider. You earn points for correct outcomes and bonuses for exact scores; consecutive correct predictions build your streak. Leaderboards reset on weekly and monthly cycles.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile and all associated data — predictions, group memberships, push tokens, and achievements. You can also email support@scorepion.fans to request deletion.

How do I turn notifications on or off?

Manage match reminders and game updates in your device's notification settings for Scorepion, or within the app's settings. You can opt out at any time.

What data does Scorepion collect?

Only what's needed to run the game — your username, email, predictions, and gameplay stats. We don't collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera, and we never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
