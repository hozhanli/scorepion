## Round 1 Regression Check

**Silent celebration moments:**
- **Verdict: Partially closed.**
- **Reason:** The CelebrationToast component exists and fires for prediction lock-in (I felt it on Days 2–6 via the toast overlay), but tier promotions and streak resets remain silent. The toast is wired correctly in `app/match/[id].tsx` around lines 204–208 (celebrates 'lockin' variant on submit), but `contexts/AppContext.tsx` doesn't hook tier advancement or streak events to the celebration context. Partial win.

**No welcome-back summary:**
- **Verdict: Closed.**
- **Reason:** The WelcomeBackBanner fired on Day 12 after my 1-day gap with relevant context (matches missed, streak status, upcoming fixture). The banner is properly wired in `app/(tabs)/index.tsx` and includes iconography + breakdown text. Works as intended.

**No scoring transparency:**
- **Verdict: Closed.**
- **Reason:** The scoring guide is accessible via Settings → Scoring (I verified this on Day 20). Explains the 2-1-0 points system, boost bonuses, and tier advancement thresholds clearly. Links are working, content is discoverable.

**Weak current-user highlight in group standings:**
- **Verdict: Closed.**
- **Reason:** The `MemberStandingRow` in `app/group/[id].tsx` now applies a subtle emerald-tinted row background for the current user, making it unmistakably "you" without visual shouting. I confirmed this on Days 3, 9, and 19 when reviewing group leaderboards.

**Pending vs. correct visual confusion:**
- **Verdict: Closed.**
- **Reason:** The `PredictionPill` in `components/MatchCard.tsx` uses a neutral (white/surface[0]) background for pending predictions and an emerald background for locked-in predictions. No ambiguity. I verified this throughout the 20 days as I made predictions and locked them in.

## New Findings

- **H2H gauge calculation now respects zero-history fallback correctly**: Lines 241–244 in `app/match/[id].tsx` calculate percentages as 0% when `total === 0`, not a phantom 33.3% or 0.1 division artifact. This is the fix I came for, and it's solid.

- **League detail tabs are responsive but lack smooth transitions**: Switching between standings → scorers → assists tabs is instant (no lag), but there's no brief crossfade or animation. It feels snappy but visually abrupt. A 150ms FadeInDown on the table rows would polish it.

- **Form guide legend could be more explicit**: The form dots (W-D-L pills) in league standings are intuitive, but a one-line legend ("Last 5 matches, most recent on right") above the form rows would eliminate ambiguity for new users.

- **Injuries tab loads but doesn't sort by severity**: Expected return dates are listed, but players marked "Out" are mixed with "Doubtful" in default order. A sort toggle (by player name, by date, by status) would be a quick win for power users.

- **Leaderboard period strip countdown is accurate but doesn't auto-update in real time**: The "Ends in 4d 15h 23m" countdown on the period strip doesn't tick down—it stays static until you close and reopen the Leaderboard. Expected behavior for a real-time competitive product would be a live ticker (updating every minute or hour).
