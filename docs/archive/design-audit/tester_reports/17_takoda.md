## Round 1 Regression Check

### 1. Silent celebration moments
**Closed.** The `CelebrationToast` component fires on lock-in (checkmark + "Locked in!"), tier promotion (trophy + "Tier X unlocked!"), points earned (star + "+10 pts"), and achievement unlocks. The toast is wired into `app/match/[id].tsx` on the predict submission, `contexts/AppContext.tsx` on tier promotions, and `components/MatchCard.tsx` on match settlement. Each variant uses the correct icon (from `DEFAULT_ICON` map) and haptic success hit. The toast is 2.4s dwell + FadeInUp entrance/exit animation. **The celebration is now audible (vibration) and visible (emerald pill).** No longer silent.

### 2. No welcome-back summary
**Closed.** The `WelcomeBackBanner` component fires when:
- User's last visit was >6 hours ago, AND
- At least one prediction has settled since then.
The banner shows: "±N points while you were away" (positive case with trending-up icon) or "N prediction(s) settled" (neutral case with time icon). Includes a subtitle with hours/days away + "tap to see how it went." The banner is dismissible and non-intrusive. It appears once per session at the top of the Home screen (wrapped in `app/(tabs)/index.tsx`). Superbru's welcome-back is more intrusive; Scorepion's is calm and respectful. **Regression closed.**

### 3. No scoring transparency
**Closed.** The `app/scoring.tsx` screen is fully implemented and discoverable from Settings → "How scoring works." It covers:
- Exact score (+10 pts)
- Correct result (+5 pts)
- Wrong result (0 pts, streak keeps)
- Boost (×2 on one daily match)
- Streak (daily prediction requirement)
- Weekly leaderboard (resets Sunday UTC)

Plus a worked example: "Arsenal 2–1 Chelsea: predict 2–1 = +10, predict 3–1 = +5, predict 1–2 = 0." The page uses Ionicons, emerald accents, hairline cards, and white background. No hidden formulas. **Regression closed.**

### 4. Weak current-user highlight in group standings
**Closed.** The `MemberStandingRow` in `app/group/[id].tsx` now includes:
- Left emerald rail (6px wide, full height)
- Emerald-tinted background (`rgba(0, 166, 81, 0.09)`)
- Emerald border (2px instead of 1px)
- Extra left padding to accommodate the rail
- Emerald-colored username + points value

When `isCurrentUser={true}`, the row is visually unmissable. Compared to group members' rows (plain white + hairline), the contrast is clear. **Regression closed.**

### 5. Pending vs correct visual confusion
**Closed.** The `PredictionPill` in `components/MatchCard.tsx` now differentiates:
- **Pending**: neutral `surface[2]` background, `text.secondary` label, lock-closed icon, "Locked 2–1" copy.
- **Exact**: `rgba(0, 166, 81, 0.10)` background, emerald text, star icon, "Exact +10" label.
- **Correct result**: `rgba(0, 166, 81, 0.10)` background, emerald text, checkmark-circle icon, "Correct +5" label.
- **Miss**: neutral `surface[2]` background, `text.tertiary` label, close-circle icon, "No points" label.

The pending state uses a lock glyph and neutral styling, so it's clearly distinct from "correct" (which uses emerald). No confusion. **Regression closed.**

## New Findings

- **Boost counter clarity**: The boosted match shows a star icon, but no "(1/1)" counter. Superbru shows "Boost: 1 left." Scorepion could add this in the match card or in a tooltip.

- **Nearest-competitor engagement gap**: The leaderboard doesn't show "3 pts ahead of you" or "gap to leader." Superbru surfaces this prominently. It's a retention lever — drives players to close the gap.

- **H2H / prediction-split viz missing**: Match detail doesn't show "Group average prediction vs. my prediction vs. actual." Superbru shows this on live matches. Not broken, but a missed engagement moment.

- **Group chat / social absence**: No in-app messaging for group banter. Superbru has a group chat tab. Scorepion is prediction-only, which is fine, but it reduces the social stickiness.

- **Streak reset notification**: When a streak resets, the app doesn't pop a toast or banner. You have to navigate to Profile to confirm. Predictor pops a gray "Streak reset" info toast. Scorepion could add this.

- **Scoring guide discoveryability**: The guide is great, but tucked in Settings/Gear. First-time players won't find it. Could add a question-mark icon to match cards or a link from the leaderboard header.

**Overall verdict:** Scorepion is now **a confident design.** The five Round 1 regressions are closed. The celebration moments feel alive, the scoring is transparent, and the group experience is clear. The app reads as intentional and premium — Emerald Minimalism is the right thesis. Weaknesses are mostly around **engagement levers** (group chat, nearest competitor, earned warnings) and **discoverability** (scoring guide), not design execution. Compared to Superbru and Sorare, Scorepion is leaner and calmer — it's not trying to be everything, and that's a strength.
