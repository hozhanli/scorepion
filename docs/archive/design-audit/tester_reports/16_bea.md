## Round 1 Regression Check

### 1. Silent celebration moments
**Closed.** The `CelebrationToast` fires on every prediction lock-in with a gentle 2400ms emerald toast + success haptic. It's visible, it's warm, it's not loud. The queue behavior (Day 6, 13) shows that even under high-frequency predictions, the toast never becomes annoying or overlapped. The design successfully walks the line between "silence = bad" and "constant celebration = fatiguing."

### 2. No welcome-back summary
**Closed.** The `WelcomeBackBanner` appeared on Day 12 after a 24h absence, showing "+8 points while you were away" in a calm emerald-tinted banner. It was dismissible, factual, and didn't use manipulative language like "we missed you!" The tone felt like a helpful summary, not guilt-tripping. The banner respected my session and didn't re-appear after dismissal.

### 3. No scoring transparency
**Closed.** The Scoring guide (accessible via Settings) clearly explains exact (+10), result (+5), no points (0), tier bonuses, etc. I read it on Day 15 and Day 24, and it answered every question. The guide link is discoverable and well-positioned. Transparency is achieved without overwhelming the main UI.

### 4. Weak current-user highlight in group standings
**Partially closed.** The `MemberStandingRow` (my row on the leaderboard) wasn't visually distinct from other rows on Days 7, 12, 14. I had to look at the name to confirm it was me. A subtle emerald background tint or a small "You" label would make it obviously me without being garish. The row exists and shows my correct data, but it doesn't jump out.

### 5. Pending vs. correct visual confusion
**Closed.** On Days 3–24, the `PredictionPill` states were clear: pending = neutral gray with lock icon ("Locked 2–1"), correct result = emerald tint with checkmark ("Correct +5"), exact = emerald tint with star ("Exact +10"), miss = neutral gray with close icon ("No points"). No confusion. The neutral gray for pending is visually distinct from the emerald success state.

## New Findings

- **Toast dismissal speed is perfect.** At 2400ms, the toast doesn't feel rushed, but it also doesn't linger long enough to distract from subsequent actions. The spring-damped entry (FadeInUp with 320ms + springify + damping 14) is warm without being bouncy.

- **WelcomeBackBanner should highlight "positive" scenarios (pointsEarned > 0) more visually.** The emerald tint (5% overlay, 30% border) on Day 12 was there, but it was very subtle. On a day when you earned +25 points, a slightly more pronounced emerald highlight would feel rewarding without being loud.

- **Streak reset doesn't fire any visual or haptic feedback.** On Day 4, the streak counter just went from 1 to 0 silently. A single frame of slight scale-down or a 100ms fade would make the change more perceptible. Not urgent, but worth considering.

- **The weekly challenge progress bar (visible on Day 14) is well-designed but could be slightly more visible on the home tab.** It's there, but easy to miss. A subtle emerald highlight or a count badge would help users stay aware without becoming naggy.

- **Boost usage should fire a small toast or inline confirmation.** On Day 7 and later, when I used the daily boost, it disappeared from the button but no toast said "Boost applied." I had to infer it from the MatchCard changing appearance. A tiny "Boost applied" in-line message or a brief toast would close that loop.
